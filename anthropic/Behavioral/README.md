# Behavioral / Culture Questions

Based on recent interview experiences.

### Questions
1.  **Altruism:**
    - "Have you done something altruistic (not for self-benefit)?"
    - Focus on mentorship, open source contribution, helping others in the community without expecting return.

2.  **Why AI / Why Anthropic?**
    - "AI is risky, why work on it?"
    - Anthropic specific: Mention **Safety**, **Alignment**, and **Constitutional AI**.
    - Emphasize the mission of building *helpful, harmless and honest* AI.

3.  **Standard Behavioral:**
    - "What was your most challenging project?"
    - "What was your most impactful project?"
    - Use the **STAR** method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
